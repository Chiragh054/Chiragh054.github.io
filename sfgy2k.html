Chapter 3: Procedure to create the product

-Use the double-sided tape to stick the dustbin, car, bucket and the garden onto the tray.

-Drill 3 holes in the dustbin as close to the other objects

-The holes that are being drilled has to be the same size as the pipe in order to get the perfect fit into the dustbin.

-To the closest distance use the board pins and pierce the tray (the needle of the pin should be outside of the tray)

-Cut the pipe and insert one side into the dustbin and the other onto the object (the length depends on the other object and the distance between the objects)

-Use unwanted plastic and cut three squares and pierce a hole at the top of the square.

-Use the plyer and cut the safety pin and tie it using the thread.

-Insert into the plastic square.

-Now wind the thread on top of the board pin and release the plastic square at the length which would cover the hole from the inside of the dustbin.
